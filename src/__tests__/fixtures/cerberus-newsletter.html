The State of CSS in Email, 2026

What changed, what still breaks, and what you can finally use.

Every year we audit the top 20 email clients against the full CSS spec. This year’s results are encouraging — and surprising.

Gmail quietly rolled out background-image support in August 2023, and Outlook Web now handles box-shadow. But Outlook Windows’s Word engine remains the elephant in the inbox.

of CSS properties are now supported across 8+ major clients

The biggest gap? Layout. Flexbox works in 7 of 12 clients, grid in only 3. Tables are still the only universal layout primitive.
